VOID FREQ is a precision ambient sound studio built for focus, flow, and deep work.

Five channels. Zero distractions.

FOCUS
Brown noise + 40Hz gamma entrainment. Scientifically associated with sustained concentration. The baseline for any serious session.

RAIN
Layered rainfall with sub-bass rumble and natural variation. Masks office noise. Dissolves mental chatter.

VOID
Five pure sine oscillators at sub-bass frequencies (30–120Hz). An isolation chamber for the mind. Nothing else.

LO-FI
A full lo-fi beat engine running in real time. Jazz chords, analog-style kick and hihat, vinyl crackle, tape warble LFO. Warm and human.

NEURONS
Binaural beats at 10Hz alpha differential. Left ear 200Hz, right ear 210Hz. Wear headphones. Let your brain synchronize.

HOW IT WORKS
Every sound is generated live using the Web Audio API — no streaming, no server, no subscription. Works offline. Continues in background. Your battery thanks you.
